Food Rescue App – UI/UX Design

Our team designed a high-fidelity prototype for a Food Rescue App focused on reducing food insecurity in Canada. The goal was to create a simple, user-friendly mobile platform that connects donors of surplus food with organizations and individuals in need.

My Role: I was part of the UI/UX team, contributing to wireframes, user flows, accessibility considerations, and ensuring that the final design aligns with real-world use cases and inclusive design practices.
